Product Delivery

Zinc combines user-centered design, customer development, lean principles, and rigorous software development practices to deliver software that is adaptable to your customers and businesses.

Training

We use our experience as practitioners and educators to provide training based on real-world problem solving, covering cutting-edge techniques and technologies as well as well-proven ones.

Coaching

We integrate directly with your team to build the skills your team needs for it's specific context. Our experience in each role required to deliver quality software gives us the context to help you discover and apply the right principles and practices for your situation.

Apprenticeship Consulting

As professional educators and practitoioners we're uniquely equipped to help teams learn how to hire competent beginners, provide ongoing mentorship, and set them up for success.

We help your team equip new hires with the specific skillsets necessary to be highly-competent at your work without taking away from your delivery focus.

Micro-projects

Our Micro-project services allow us to take a well-defined valuable-yet-hard problem and turn it into working software without a long-term commitment. Need to instrument your application for analytics? Set up continuous integration? Bring your devops tool-chain under control? Get a particularly gnarly bit of code under test? Build a proof of concept integrating complex APIs? We're your team.

Minimum Viable Value Propositions

Have a business idea you want to test? We build out a rapid prototype and organize customer development and user research sessions to validate your hypothesis.

Once we've proven there's a market, we begin product delivery; ensuring your business has a rock-solid foundation to serve it's customers.
